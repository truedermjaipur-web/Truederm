Meet the Doctor
Dr. Iftekhar Khan
MBBS, MD Dermatology | AIIMS Delhi
🏛️ AIIMS Delhi

Dr. Iftekhar Khan is a dedicated and experienced dermatologist known for his precise diagnosis and patient-focused approach. He specializes in skin, hair, laser, and aesthetic treatments, offering solutions that are safe, effective, and tailored to individual needs.

With strong clinical expertise and exposure to advanced dermatological practices, Dr. Khan focuses on identifying the root cause of each concern rather than relying on temporary fixes. His areas of expertise include acne, pigmentation, hair loss, anti-ageing treatments, Botox, and advanced procedures like hair transplant and laser therapies.

At Truederm, he follows a personalized treatment approach where every patient receives a customized plan based on their skin type, condition, and lifestyle. His goal is not just to treat the concern but to improve overall skin health and restore confidence.

Dr. Khan believes in maintaining natural-looking results and combining medical science with aesthetic precision. His commitment to patient care, transparency, and long-term results makes him a trusted name in dermatology.
